Short Bio

I am an experimental researcher and a full-stack engineer, currently, a postdoc researcher working with Prof. Longfei Shangguan, on mobile system research, at University of Pittsburgh. Prior to my postdoc, I received my PhD from City University of Hong Kong, under the supervision of Prof. Zhenjiang Li.

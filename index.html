Short Bio

I have completed my PhD study at the Department of Computer Science, City University of Hong Kong, under the supervision of Dr. Zhenjiang Li. My broad research interests lie in the areas of mobile computing, signal processing and embedded system design. My vision is to develop human-centric communication, networking, sensing and computation technologies on commercial mobile, wearable and customized hardware devices for strengthening human convenience, intelligence, health and safety in our society. Currently, I focuses on developing novel systems and applications interacted with acoustic signals and things, ranges from earable, voice-controlled interfaces, to commodity smartphones. I also work very closely with Dr. Longfei Shangguan at Mircosoft.
